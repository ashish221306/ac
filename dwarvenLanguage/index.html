The Dwarven Language Translator

Dwarven of LOTC

This is a rough translator for the language of the dwarves within the world of the Minecraft Roleplay server, Lord of the Craft, or, LOTC. Due to the overall lack of use-able words in the official thread regarding the Dwarven language on LOTC forums, there are essential words in this translator that are not part of that post. This is so should a player wish to use the translator to speak dwarven in game, there won't be random common words littered throughout.
